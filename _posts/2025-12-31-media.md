### Basic image markup

```liquid
<img
  class="rf-media"
  src="{{ '/assets/img/crt.gif' | relative_url }}"
  alt="CRT animation"
  loading="lazy"
  decoding="async"
>
````

---

### Figure + caption (live)

<figure class="rf-figure">
  <img
    class="rf-media"
    src="{{ '/assets/img/crt_nature.gif' | relative_url }}"
    alt="Sample image description."
    loading="lazy"
    decoding="async"
  >
  <figcaption class="rf-caption">
    A caption that reads like print: clear, short, and descriptive.
  </figcaption>
</figure>

### Figure markup

<figure class="rf-figure">
